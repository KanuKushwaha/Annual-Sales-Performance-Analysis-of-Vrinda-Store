Vrinda Store Sales Analysis & Dashboard (2022)
Project Overview

This project analyzes the 2022 sales data of Vrinda Store to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and overall business performance. An interactive Excel dashboard is developed to support data-driven decision-making and improve sales strategy for 2023.

Objective
Analyze customer purchasing patterns
Identify top-performing regions and sales channels
Evaluate monthly sales trends
Generate actionable business insights
Dataset

The dataset includes:

Order details
Customer demographics (age, gender)
Sales amount
Order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
Sales channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)
State-wise sales data
Dashboard Features
Monthly Sales vs Orders trend analysis
Gender-wise sales distribution
Order status breakdown
Top 10 states contributing to sales
Age and gender relationship analysis
Channel-wise sales contribution
Interactive filters (Month, Category, Channel)
Key Insights
Women contribute approximately 64% of total sales
The 30–49 age group contributes around 50% of total orders
Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top-performing states
Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra contribute nearly 80% of total sales
92% of orders are successfully delivered
Business Recommendations
Focus on women customers aged 30–49 years
Increase marketing efforts in top-performing states
Prioritize high-performing sales channels
Plan campaigns around peak sales months
Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts and Visualizations
Slicers and Filters
Dashboard Design
Deliverables
Interactive Excel Dashboard
Data Analysis Report
Business Insights and Recommendations
Conclusion

This project highlights how structured data analysis and visualization can support effective business decisions. By targeting the right customer segments and optimizing sales channels, Vrinda Store can improve its overall performance.
